About Us

Welcome to **Ellie Velly Interactive**, a passionate Roblox game development studio dedicated to crafting engaging, innovative, and fun experiences for players worldwide!

Founded by Ellie Velly, our mission is to build vibrant virtual worlds and create games that inspire creativity, challenge skills, and provide endless entertainment. We specialize in exciting Tycoon games, like our popular titles **Pin Perfect Tycoon** and **Plane Merge Tycoon**, and are constantly working on new adventures for you to explore.

Join our community and be part of the journey as we continue to push the boundaries of fun on Roblox!
